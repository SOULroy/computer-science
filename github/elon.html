About Elon Musk

Elon Musk is a business magnate, industrial designer, and engineer. He is the founder, CEO, and lead designer of SpaceX; early investor, CEO, and product architect of Tesla, Inc.; owner and CEO of Neuralink; and founder of The Boring Company. He is known for his ambitious vision of the future and has been involved in various groundbreaking projects.

SpaceX

Elon Musk's aerospace manufacturer and space transportation company, SpaceX, is known for its ambitious missions and goal to make life multi-planetary. Some of SpaceX's notable achievements include launching reusable rockets and the development of the Starship spacecraft.

Tesla, Inc.

Tesla, Inc. is a pioneering electric vehicle and clean energy company founded by Elon Musk. Tesla's electric cars and renewable energy products have transformed the automotive and energy industries.
